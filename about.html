About Velox

Built by chemists, run like an assembly line

Velox started in a two-reactor pilot facility with a simple thesis: most manufacturing partners promise purity and deliver drift. We built our entire operation — from R&D to logistics — around eliminating batch-to-batch variance.

Today our production facility runs 24 continuous and batch reactor lines, staffed by process engineers who treat every specification sheet as a contract, not a suggestion.

2010

Pilot facility founded

Two-reactor lab producing custom small-batch compounds for research partners.

2014

ISO 9001 certification

Formalized quality systems across synthesis, testing and packaging lines.

2019

Plant expansion

Scaled to 120,000 tons annual capacity across three production halls.

2024

Zero-waste solvent loop

Closed-loop recovery system cut solvent waste across all reactor lines by 86%.

Our mission

Deliver predictable chemistry at every scale

We aim to make complex manufacturing feel repeatable and reliable for our customers, from the first laboratory batch through industrial shipment.

Why reliability matters

Every formulation we produce is designed for consistency. That means fewer line stops, fewer deviations and greater confidence in customer production schedules.

How we support partners

We pair chemical engineers with manufacturing and compliance experts so partners get both a technical recipe and a production-ready execution plan.
